## Overview

Modify your dashboard with **Fan Control**! This add-on provides an intuitive interface for managing your smart fans directly from your dashboard. Control fan speed, toggle power, and monitor status with ease through beautifully designed cards that integrate seamlessly with your Home Assistant setup.

Enhance your comfort and convenience with streamlined fan controls that put complete management at your fingertips.
