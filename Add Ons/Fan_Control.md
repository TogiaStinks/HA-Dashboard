# Fan Control

![Fan Control Demo](Fan_GIF_Demo.gif)

Modify your dashboard with **Fan Control**!

Take complete control of your smart fans with an intuitive and responsive interface. This add-on provides seamless fan management directly from your dashboard, allowing you to adjust speeds, toggle power states, and monitor fan status with elegant, easy-to-use controls.

## Features
- Intuitive fan speed controls
- Quick power toggle functionality
- Real-time status monitoring
- Beautifully designed interface that matches your dashboard theme
