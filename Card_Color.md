# Card Color

![Card Color Demo](Card_Color_GIF_Demo.gif)

## Overview

Modify your dashboard with **Card Color**! This feature allows you to customize the color scheme of your cards, creating a more personalized and visually appealing interface. Apply dynamic color changes to match your home's ambiance or simply to distinguish between different types of controls at a glance.

Transform your Home Assistant dashboard into a truly unique experience by adding vibrant colors that reflect your style and preferences.
