## Overview

Modify your dashboard with **Navigation Bar**! This add-on introduces a sleek, customizable navigation system that makes switching between different views and rooms effortless. The modern navigation bar provides quick access to your most important dashboard sections with smooth transitions and an intuitive layout.

Streamline your navigation experience and create a more organized, user-friendly dashboard that makes controlling your smart home faster and more enjoyable.
